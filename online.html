Various Online Courses Available

Absolutely, here are online courses offered at our computer science department:
● Machine Learning:
This course, covers fundamental machine learning concepts and algorithms.
● Algorithms, Part I & II :
Dive into foundational algorithms and data structures with these courses.
● Deep Learning Specialization:
This series explores deep learning, neural networks, and their applications.
● Cybersecurity Fundamentals:
An introductory course covering essential cybersecurity concepts and practices.
● Data Science MicroMasters Program :
This comprehensive program delves into data analysis, machine learning, and big data techniques.
● Full Stack Web Development :
Learn front-end and back-end web development skills, including databases and servers.
● Computer Networks :
Explore the principles of computer networking, protocols, and security in this intermediate-level course.
● Software Engineering for SaaS:
Focuses on software architecture, design patterns, and agile development for Software as a Service (SaaS) products.
● Introduction to Artificial Intelligence:
An introductory course covering AI concepts, including machine learning and neural networks.
● Blockchain Basics:
An overview of blockchain technology, its applications, and cryptographic principles.

At University of Ghana's Computer Science Department, we're driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence in education, research, and technological advancement.
